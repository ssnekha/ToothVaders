HOW TO PLAY TOOTHVADERS

Goal:

You start with 5 lives and 25 points. Avoid the sweets; they damage the

tooth and cause you to lose a life and 5 points! Get the floss or the

toothpaste to gain an extra life and 5 more points. Once you get to zero lives,

or if you are not able to get 10 lives (50 points) within 40 seconds, the game is over.

If you obtain 10 lives within 40 seconds you win the game!

Control Keys:

Move the tooth around the screen using up, down, left, right arrow keys.
